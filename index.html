About Me

I am Sonnet Podder, a statistician with a strong academic foundation and practical expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. I completed both my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Statistics from Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Science and Technology University in 2020 and 2021, respectively. I aim to solve real-world challenges in public health and bioinformatics. I am currently available to collaborate on research projects, particularly in the fields of bioinformatics and public health.
